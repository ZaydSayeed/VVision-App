**Vela Vision — Dementia Care Companion**

Vela Vision is a dementia care companion built for families who want to stay close, even from a distance. Whether you're a caregiver managing a loved one's day or a patient navigating your routine, Vela Vision brings calm, clarity, and connection to every moment.

**For Caregivers**
Stay informed with a live activity timeline that shows medication tracking and daily routine progress all in one place. Get instant alerts when your loved one needs help or when something in their routine changes. Manage your entire care team, link multiple caregivers to a single patient, and coordinate care without the back and forth. Our AI assistant helps you track patterns, answer questions about your patient's history, and surface what matters most.

**For Patients**
Wake up to a personalized daily routine that lays out your tasks, medications, and schedule in a simple, warm interface designed to reduce confusion. Check off medications as you take them and log how you're feeling. And if you ever need help, one tap alerts your entire care team right away.

**Key Features**
• Real-time activity timeline for caregivers
• One-tap help alerts sent to all caregivers simultaneously
• Medication tracking with progress indicators
• Daily routine and task management
• AI assistant for caregivers with full patient context
• Mood check-ins for patients
• Care team management and coordination
• Push notifications for alerts and reminders
• Dark mode support

Dementia care is hard. Vela Vision makes it a little less so.
